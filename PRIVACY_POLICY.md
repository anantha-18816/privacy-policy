# Privacy Policy for Agrimillet

**Last Updated:** March 28, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Agrimillet ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the Agrimillet mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

We use your data to provide and improve our Service. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information Collection and Use

We collect and use the following types of information:

### 2.1 Account Information
- Name and email address
- Phone number
- Password (securely hashed)
- Profile picture (optional)
- User type (Farmer or Buyer)

### 2.2 Location Data
- GPS coordinates (for delivery tracking and GPS map services)
- Approximate location based on address
- Location data is collected only when you enable location services or explicitly request GPS tracking features

### 2.3 Order Information
- Delivery address
- Order history
- Products ordered
- Order status and tracking information

### 2.4 Payment Information
- Payment method information (processed securely by Razorpay)
- We do NOT store complete credit card or UPI details
- Payment data is encrypted and handled by our payment processor

### 2.5 Device Information
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- App version
- Device language and locale

### 2.6 Voice Data
- Voice/audio input for voice assistant features
- Voice data is processed in real-time and NOT persistently stored
- Used only to process your voice commands
- Transmitted securely to speech recognition services

### 2.7 Usage Information
- Pages and features accessed
- Time and duration of use
- Clicks and interactions
- Error reports and performance metrics
- Firebase Analytics events

### 2.8 Communication Data
- Messages sent through the in-app support system
- Support tickets and inquiries
- Feedback and reviews

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

1. **Service Delivery**
   - Create and maintain your account
   - Process and fulfill orders
   - Provide GPS tracking and delivery updates
   - Enable voice assistant functionality

2. **Communication**
   - Send order confirmations and updates
   - Provide customer support
   - Send notifications about promotions (with your consent)
   - Respond to your inquiries

3. **Improvement & Analytics**
   - Improve app features and performance
   - Analyze usage patterns
   - Develop new features
   - Conduct research and troubleshooting

4. **Safety & Security**
   - Detect and prevent fraud
   - Enforce terms of service
   - Protect user rights and safety
   - Comply with legal obligations

5. **Marketing** (with your consent)
   - Send promotional offers
   - Inform you about new features
   - Conduct marketing surveys

## 4. Location Data

### 4.1 GPS Tracking
- Location data is used exclusively for real-time order tracking
- Shared with delivery partners only during active delivery
- Automatically deleted 30 days after order completion
- You can disable location services anytime through app settings

### 4.2 Address Information
- Delivery addresses are stored for order fulfillment
- Used to calculate delivery fees and time estimates
- Retained until you delete your account

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 Third-Party Service Providers
We may share your information with:

- **Razorpay** - Payment processing and security
- **Firebase** - Authentication, database, and analytics
- **Google Maps** - Location and mapping services
- **Google Cloud** - Data storage and processing
- **Speech-to-Text Services** - Voice recognition processing

### 5.2 Farmer-Buyer Interactions
- Buyers can see farmer profiles and crop listings
- Farmers can see buyer information during orders
- Contact information shared only with consent for order fulfillment

### 5.3 Legal Obligations
We may disclose personal information if required by law or in good faith belief that such action is necessary to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights and safety
- Prevent fraud or misuse
- Protect user safety

### 5.4 No Commercial Sale
We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

## 6. Data Security

### 6.1 Security Measures
- **Encryption**: All data transmitted using HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **Authentication**: Secure Firebase authentication with password hashing
- **Access Control**: Limited employee access to personal data
- **Regular Audits**: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
- **Secure Storage**: Data stored in secure Google Cloud infrastructure

### 6.2 Data Protection
- Payment data handled by PCI-DSS compliant Razorpay
- Voice data processed securely and not stored long-term
- No sensitive information stored in app local storage without encryption

### 6.3 Limitations
While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

## 7. Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained while account is active, deleted upon account deletion
- **Order History**: Retained for 2 years for transaction records
- **Location Data**: Deleted 30 days after delivery completion
- **Voice Data**: Not persistently stored, deleted after processing
- **Support Communications**: Retained for 1 year for reference
- **Device Information**: Retained for service analytics (anonymized after 90 days)

## 8. Your Rights and Choices

### 8.1 Access and Portability
- You can access your personal data anytime in your profile settings
- Request a copy of your data in portable format

### 8.2 Correction and Deletion
- Update your profile information anytime through app settings
- Request deletion of your account and associated data
- Deletion will be processed within 30 days

### 8.3 Opt-Out Options
- Disable location services in device settings or app settings
- Disable push notifications anytime
- Opt-out of promotional emails
- Disable analytics (limited functionality may result)

### 8.4 Withdrawal of Consent
- Withdraw consent for data collection anytime
- Some features may not work without certain data

### 8.5 Do Not Track
- We do not respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals
- You can control tracking through app and device settings

## 9. Children's Privacy

Agrimillet is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information and terminate the child's account.

For users 13-18:
- Parental consent may be required in some jurisdictions
- Limited data collection and targeted features
- Additional privacy protections
